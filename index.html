Luca Mannella

Postdoctoral Researcher & Research Assistant
Politecnico di Torino

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher and Research Assistant in the Department of Control and Computer Engineering (DAUIN) at Politecnico di Torino, member of the SMILIES research group. My research focuses on cybersecurity, Internet of Things (IoT), and software security, with a particular emphasis on automotive cybersecurity and securing IoT gateways in smart home environments.

Research Interests

  • Cybersecurity and IoT security
  • Software engineering and secure software development
  • Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) in security
  • Automotive cybersecurity
  • Edge computing security
